### THE PROBLEM: THE $100M AMNESIA
Current AI models (LLMs) are static. Updating them requires full retraining.
* **Economic Waste:** Retraining a 7B+ model costs >$200k in compute.
* **Catastrophic Forgetting:** Learning task B destroys the performance on task A.
* **Strategic Risk:** European SMEs cannot afford the infrastructure for continuous updates.

### THE SOLUTION: GATED GHOST EXPANSION
We replace destructive retraining with additive architectural expansion.
* **Ghost Parameters:** Latent, orthogonal sub-spaces that only activate for new data.
* **Frozen Core:** The base model remains mathematically untouched (0% Forgetting Rate).
* **Green AI:** Updates consume **90% less energy** than retraining.
